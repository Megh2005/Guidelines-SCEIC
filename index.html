Submission of Research Papers

Formatting Guidelines

  • Use 1-inch margins on all sides.
  • Double space throughout the text.
  • Choose a standard font like Times New Roman, 12 pt.
  • Left-align text (don't justify).
  • Indent first line of paragraphs (except title page).
  • Center titles and headings with proper capitalization.
  • Include page numbers consistently (often top right).
  • Follow a specific citation style (e.g., APA, MLA).
  • Create a separate References or Works Cited page.
  • Double-space and alphabetize references in the list.

Plagiarism Guidelines

  • Strive for original wording, even when referencing existing ideas.
  • Restate information in your own words, demonstrating comprehension.
  • Give credit for any borrowed ideas or data, regardless of format.
  • Use quotations for specific wording you can't rephrase effectively.
  • Facts widely considered public knowledge typically don't require citation.
  • Cite your own previous work if you are reusing content that for references.
  • Clearly distinguish quoted material from your own writing.
  • Limited copying for critique or commentary might be permissible.
  • Referencing styles and plagiarism policies can vary with respect to credit claiming.
  • We will run your paper through plagiarism tracking software to identify potential issues.
